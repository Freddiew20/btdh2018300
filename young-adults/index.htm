Young Adults

Hillside's Young Adult Ministry is a gathering of young adults that meets weekly to be an intentional community as we work through what it means to follow Jesus at school, at work, and with our families & friends.

When We Gather

Weekly Gathering | Mondays 7:30 p.m. in the Fireside Room (Coffee & Hangout Starts at 7 p.m.)

Young Adults at Hillside is for those who are looking for a place to worship and connect on weekly basis. This is a night of worship, teaching, and community that always includes free food and coffee.
